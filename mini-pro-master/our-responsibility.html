OUR RESPONSIBILITY

  
Building strong, skilled and sustainable communities.














We seek freedom and adventure and are known for positively impacting the
communities where we live, work and ride. So while Harley-Davidson can be found in
all corners of the world, we are firmly rooted in the places we call home and deeply
connected to the people who are our friends and neighbors.
COMMUNITY
We help fulfill dreams through partnerships that strengthen and enrich our
communities.
Through the company, The Harley-Davidson Foundation, our dealers, employees and
customers, Harley-Davidson has a long history of supporting communities and
causes. We seek to support positive social impact for our communities, employees and riders.
Wherever we have employees, we have a passion for giving back. Employee-led
charitable contribution committees across the globe target the areas of giving that
will have the most impact locally.
We also have a passion for volunteering, and our employees go above and beyond to
make the world a better place. In the U.S., employee volunteer hours are matched
with a monetary donation to help our neighbors even more.
Our riders are passionate about giving back too, whether it’s a H.O.G.® chapter
charity ride, our long-standing support of Muscular Dystrophy Association, the bond
we share with those who serve, or our support of breast cancer causes through Pink
Label apparel.
SUSTAINABILITYs
To achieve our long-term sustainability objective to grow
our business without growing our environmental impact,
we’re focused on reducing our impacts across the entire
value chain – from product design decisions and material
selections to energy consumption in our manufacturing
and distribution operations. 
This focus is leading to new levels of innovation for delivering personal freedom to
our customers without compromising the performance and experience expected
from Harley-Davidson.
To learn more about Harley-Davidson’s sustainability efforts, download the current
Sustainability Report.
 Harley-Davidson has published annual Sustainability Reports since 2009. To obtain
copies of earlier reports, please contact SustainabilityReports@Harley-
Davidson.com.
RENEW THE RIDE™
Renew the Ride is our initiative to increase positive social
and environmental impact by inspiring Harley-Davidson
employees, dealers, customers and fans to plant trees
where we live, ride and work and in forests around the
world.
Trees not only provide valuable ecosystem services and societal health benefits, they
provide the foundation of the beautiful places we love to live in, ride through and
visit – whether urban or rural, parkland, farmland or fully developed. In recognition
of how trees make the world a better place to live – and a better place to ride free –
we have established an ambitious long-term goal of planting 50 million trees through
this initiative.
